Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trendpilot (the "Service"), operated by an independent individual developer ("we", "us", "our"), handles your information. We respect your privacy and collect only what is necessary to operate the Service on your behalf.

1. Information we receive

When you sign in to Trendpilot using TikTok's Login Kit, TikTok provides the Service with an access token and a limited set of information about your own TikTok account, scoped to the permissions you grant. We may receive:

Source / scopeWhat we receiveWhy
Login Kit · user.info.basicYour open ID, display name, avatar URLTo identify your account inside the dashboard.
Display API · user.info.profile, user.info.statsYour bio, follower count, like countTo show your own profile summary.
Display API · video.listList of your own posted videos and their performance metricsTo show you analytics of your own content.
Content Posting API · video.upload, video.publishThe videos you choose to upload through the ServiceTo deliver your video to TikTok at the time you specify.

2. How we use information

We use this information solely to operate the Service for you: showing your dashboard, scheduling and publishing your own videos, and displaying analytics about your own content. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

3. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store data about other TikTok users beyond what is necessary to display publicly available trend information surfaced by TikTok's own APIs.
  • We do not collect your TikTok password. Authentication is handled entirely by TikTok via OAuth.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising SDKs.

4. Storage and retention

Access tokens and the minimum profile fields needed to operate your dashboard are stored encrypted at rest. Videos you upload through the Service are stored only long enough to deliver them to TikTok; once TikTok confirms upload, the source file is deleted from our servers. You can request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us (see "Contact" below).

5. Your choices

  • You can revoke Trendpilot's access to your TikTok account at any time from your TikTok settings → Manage account → Connected apps.
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing us.
  • You can stop using the Service at any time.

6. Security

We use HTTPS for all traffic between your browser and the Service, encrypted storage for credentials, and limit access to data to the operator of the Service. No internet service can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we apply industry-standard practices.

7. Children

Trendpilot is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from such children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review the policy periodically.
